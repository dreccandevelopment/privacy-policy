Privacy Policy for FocusFlow

Effective date: September 11, 2025

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how FocusFlow (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles user data in compliance with Google Play policies.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: FocusFlow does not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., contacting us via email for support).
  • Device Data: The app and its third-party partners (such as Google AdMob) may collect non-personal information including device type, operating system, app usage, advertising identifiers (e.g., AAID), and interactions with ads.
  • Sensitive Data: FocusFlow does not collect sensitive data such as financial, health, or biometric information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user information. However, we use third-party services such as Google AdMob to display ads. These services may collect and process data (including device identifiers and app interactions) in order to provide personalized or non-personalized ads and to prevent fraud. Data may also be shared when required by law.

How We Use Information

Any collected data is used solely for:

  • Improving app functionality and user experience.
  • Providing customer support when requested.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user information. Data may be shared only when required by law or with trusted third-party services (such as analytics or crash reporting), always in compliance with Google Play policies.

Children’s Privacy

FocusFlow is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain user data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Users may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
